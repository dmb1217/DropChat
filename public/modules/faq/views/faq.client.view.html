Frequently Asked Questions

How do I drop a message?

After you create an account with us, you can click anywhere on the map and type your message in the pop-up window.

How can I edit my messages?

To edit a message you’ve already submitted, navigate to your posts page from the navigation bar. You’ll find an “edit” and a “delete” button to the right of their corresponding posts.

How can I see more posts from another user?

Once you click on the map to view another user’s message, click on that user’s name to view their profile.

Can I search for a location?

Yes. A search bar can be found beneath the map. Just type in an address and the map will refresh to show you that location.

I don’t want others to see my real name. How do I change this?

Use the navigation bar to go to your profile page. From there, you can change your name to whatever you’d like to be displayed on your messages.
